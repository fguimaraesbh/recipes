Ingredients:
- 2 or 3 nice field-type mushrooms
- olive oil

Directions:
1. Quarter or roughly chop the mushrooms and add to a hot frying pan with a 
small knob of butter, a drizzle of olive oil and a pinch of salt and pepper. 
2. Fry and toss around until golden, then turn the heat down to medium.
3. Add your eggs and move the pan around to spread them out evenly. 
4. When the omelette begins to cook and firm up, but still has a little raw egg 
on top, sprinkle over the Cheddar. 
5. Continue as for the basic omelette
